Charles-Alexis Ruzette
I am an architecture student based between Brussels and Ghent, Belgium. I am currently completing a Master of Architecture at KU Leuven, having previously earned a Bachelor of Architecture with Distinction from UCLouvain LOCI Brussels.

My work explores the relationship between dwelling and urban life, with a particular interest in collective space, thresholds, and the ways architecture shapes everyday encounters. Alongside my academic work, I am interested in how different cultural contexts shape architecture and everyday life, a perspective I look forward to further exploring during my Erasmus exchange at Waseda University in Tokyo.

In 2026, I contributed drawings to Marta Malinverni's article What If Hestia Left the Fire?, published by Burning Farm.
