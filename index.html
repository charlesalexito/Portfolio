Charles-Alexis Ruzette
Charles-Alexis Ruzette (Belgium, 2004) is studying architecture at KU Leuven, Ghent, where he is completing a Master of Architecture. He holds a Bachelor of Architecture from UCLouvain Saint-Luc in Brussels. His work is concerned with collective space, threshold, and the relationship between dwelling and urban life. He is preparing for an Erasmus semester in Tokyo. He contributed drawings to "What If Hestia Left the Fire?" by Marta Malinverni, published in Burning Farm in 2026.
