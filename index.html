Charles-Alexis Ruzette
I am an architecture student based between Brussels and Ghent, Belgium. I am currently completing a Master of Architecture at KU Leuven, having previously earned a Bachelor of Architecture with Distinction from UCLouvain LOCI Brussels.

My work explores the relationship between dwelling and urban life, with a particular interest in collective space, thresholds, and the ways architecture shapes everyday encounters. Drawing from both European and Japanese architectural culture, my projects often investigate questions of density, domesticity, and spatial ambiguity.

In 2026, I will undertake an Erasmus exchange at Waseda University in Tokyo. I contributed only the drawings to Marta Malinverni’s article What If Hestia Left the Fire?, published by Burning Farm.
