Charles-Alexis Ruzette
Charles-Alexis Ruzette (b. 2004, Belgium) is an architecture student based between Brussels and Ghent, Belgium. He is currently completing a Master of Architecture at KU Leuven, having previously earned a Bachelor of Architecture from UCLouvain LOCI Brussels.

His work explores the relationship between dwelling and urban life, with a particular interest in collective space, thresholds, and the ways architecture shapes everyday encounters. Drawing from both European and Japanese architectural culture, his projects often investigate questions of density, domesticity, and spatial ambiguity.

In 2026, he will undertake an Erasmus exchange at Waseda University in Tokyo. He also contributed drawings to What If Hestia Left the Fire? by Marta Malinverni, published by Burning Farm.
